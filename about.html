About European CLIL Academy

Empowering primary teachers with innovative CLIL methodologies for the modern European classroom.

Our Mission

European CLIL Academy provides high-quality professional development for primary school teachers who want to implement Content and Language Integrated Learning (CLIL).

Strengthen teacher confidence with evidence-based strategies.
Provide practical, classroom-ready strategies for immediate implementation.
Support meaningful and engaging learning experiences for young learners.

Our European Alignment

All our courses are fully compatible with Erasmus+ mobility projects. We provide full assistance with documentation (certificates of attendance, mobility agreements) and guarantee our courses meet European quality standards.
