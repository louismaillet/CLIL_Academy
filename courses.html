CLIL in Primary Education – Practical Strategies for the Classroom

This intensive course equips primary teachers (generalists or language specialists) with practical methodologies to teach subjects like Mathematics, Science, Arts, Music, Physical Education, History, Geography... through a foreign language.

Duration: 5 days (25 hours)
Audience: Primary Teachers
Group Size: 8–15
Language: English (minimum B1)

Learning Outcomes

  • Master scaffolding techniques for primary students.
  • Learn to integrate subject-specific vocabulary effectively.
  • Design effective lesson planning and implement micro-teaching.
  • Develop a strong ability for cross-curricular integration.

Example of Detailed Program

Day 1

Principles & Benefits of CLIL for young learners.

Day 2

Focus on STEM (Maths & Science) + Scaffolding.

Day 3

Arts & Music (Creativity, songs, and movement).

Day 4

Physical Education (Language in movement & instructions).

Day 5

Humanities (History/Geo) + Micro-teaching and Feedback.

Financial & Administrative Information

Included: Training, materials, and a Certificate of Attendance.

Location: Chartres, France (1 hour from Paris).
